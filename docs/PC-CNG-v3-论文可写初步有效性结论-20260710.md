# PC-CNG v3 论文可写的初步有效性结论

## 结论判断

当前实验已经可以证明：

```text
PC-CNG 生成并筛选出的 hard negatives 对下游 candidate product reranking 有初步有效性。
```

更具体地说：

1. 在 RegioSQM20 / HiTEA 同源数据体系上，PC-CNG negatives 提升了 observed positive product 在候选集中的排序。
2. 这种提升体现在 top-1、MRR、NDCG，而不仅仅是 binary ROC-AUC。
3. 该结果与 Science Advances 论文的核心观点一致：有信息量的 negative data 可以帮助模型识别 successful reaction manifold 的边界。

不能过度表述为：

```text
PC-CNG 已经复现或超过 Science Advances 的 Transformer + RL forward prediction。
```

原因是当前模型仍是 feasibility scorer / reranker，不是 autoregressive product prediction policy。

## 可写进论文的实验定位

建议作为论文中的一个核心实验小节：

```text
Downstream utility of generated negative reactions for candidate product reranking
```

或：

```text
PC-CNG hard negatives improve boundary-aware product candidate ranking
```

这部分可以放在主文 Results 中，而不是只放补充材料。原因是它直接回答了模型生成的负样本是否对下游任务有用。

已接入真实 Chemformer USPTO-50K forward checkpoint，并完成全量 candidate scorer benchmark。

```text

```

候选集：

```text

```

两类 scorer：

1. `chemformer_forward`：top-10 beam exact-match。候选产物只有进入 beam 才有真实 beam log-likelihood。
2. `chemformer_log_likelihood`：teacher-forced conditional log-likelihood。每个候选产物都会被打分。

结果：

| Scorer | Overall Top-1 | Overall MRR | Test Top-1 | RegioSQM20 Top-1 | Synthetic-source Top-1 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| length baseline | 0.6586 | 0.7726 | 0.6364 | 0.6194 | 0.7609 |
| Chemformer top-10 beam | 0.6631 | 0.7750 | 0.6364 | 0.6266 | 0.7645 |
| Chemformer log-likelihood | **0.6801** | **0.7793** | **0.7424** | **0.6498** | **0.9638** |

解释：

```text
Chemformer top-k beam scorer 命中率很低，仅 43 / 32,287 candidates 出现在 top-10 beams 中。
因此 beam exact-match 只能作为覆盖率诊断。
teacher-forced conditional log-likelihood 能覆盖所有候选，更适合作为论文中的 pretrained Reaction LM 证据。
```

可写结论：

```text
Using a pretrained Chemformer forward model as an external reaction language-model scorer, PC-CNG candidate sets preserve a strong distinction between observed products and generated boundary negatives under conditional product likelihood. The teacher-forced Chemformer scorer substantially improves synthetic-source candidate ranking (Top-1 = 0.9638) and improves held-out test Top-1 from the length baseline of 0.6364 to 0.7424.
```

训练逻辑：

谨慎解释：

```text
该 DPO reward model 不是普通二分类 feasibility SOTA；它的 test ROC-AUC 约为 0.7466。
```

论文可写表述：

```text
Beyond using Chemformer as a frozen evaluator, we trained a lightweight DPO-style reward model using Chemformer likelihoods as the reference policy and PC-CNG boundary negatives as rejected outcomes. This preference-tuned scorer improved held-out candidate ranking Top-1 from 0.7424 under the frozen Chemformer likelihood to 0.8636, supporting that PC-CNG negatives can be used not only for evaluation but also as actionable preference feedback.
```

```text
In a 5-seed ablation, all PC-CNG-supervised reward models substantially improved held-out candidate ranking over frozen Chemformer likelihoods. Pairwise boundary supervision was the strongest signal in the current candidate set, while Chemformer-referenced DPO provided an additional preference-learning formulation rather than a universally superior objective.
```

### Diversity-aware regio/heteroatom expansion

